This is your complimentary invite for the RIMES Annual Client and Insights Conference in New York City on September 15, 2016. Following the Conference, we will be celebrating RIMES’ 20th anniversary with our clients and partners at a party on the roof-top of the Gansevoort Park Avenue Hotel in NYC.
The conference takes place in the eastern section of Manhattan’s NoMad district. The neighborhood offers guests the chance to explore gorgeous Madison Square Park, boutique retailers such as Maison Kitsune, and a diverse range of bars featuring live jazz, noted mixologists and stand-up comedians.
Of course, NoMad isn’t the only part of NYC that has something to offer. For one thing, Fashion Week New York will be wrapping up just before the conference, so there’s a chance you’ll see some of the industry’s hottest stars. Admire the 16th century paintings at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, see an Off-Broadway show at Playwrights Horizons, or visit the National September 11th Memorial and Museum.
As you can imagine, there are more than a few hidden gems throughout the city. If you want to do something a little out of the ordinary, check out Explorers Club Headquarters on East 70th Street, the Evolution Nature Store in SoHo or the City Hall Station to get a taste of NYC’s beautiful history.
